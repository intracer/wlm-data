Comparative table of objects classified by the
KATOTTH (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Codifier_of_administrative-territorial_units_and_territories_of_territorial_communities) 
and 
KOATUU (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Classification_of_objects_of_the_administrative-territorial_system_of_Ukraine)

Source: https://decentralization.ua/uploads/attachment/document/751/%D0%9F%D0%BE%D1%80%D1%96%D0%B2%D0%BD%D1%8F%D0%BB%D1%8C%D0%BD%D0%B0_%D1%82%D0%B0%D0%B1%D0%BB%D0%B8%D1%86%D1%8F.xlsx

This mapping is needed because monuments database was initiated in 2012 while KOATTUU was in place, and KOATTUU was replaced in 2020 with a new classifier KATOTTH

Also due to territorial reform some administrative units coded in KOATUU ceased to exist and new ones coded in KATOTTH were created, 
so KATOTTH and KOATUU do no map exactly to each other.
